Dining Cabinets

A china cabinet is a classic accent piece that provides a place to display photographs, knick-knacks, and fine dinnerware. Antique china cabinets may be placed in the living room, foyer, or dining room as decorative furnishings. China cabinets are must-have pieces that complete the appearance of a room and enhance the comforting appeal of your home.

In order to achieve a welcoming atmosphere in your home, you need to add a personal touch, and there is no better way to accomplish this than to display your cherished photographs, mementos, and porcelain dinnerware in a china hutch. China cabinets and hutches provide the perfect opportunity for your home to reflect who you are as a person and create a sense of warmth in your home.

China cabinets are a stunning piece to have in your home to help display your most prized possessions that are near and dear to your heart. China style cabinets were first designed in England around the end of the 17th century with the intention to use them to display the fine china that was popular during that time. Classic styles and antique china cabinets are still available, but today’s furniture makers have also reinvented classic designs to produce modern china cabinets and hutches. LuxeDecor has a wide selection of classic and modern style cabinets for sale to suit any style or aesthetic.

Before You Buy

There are several factors that may influence your choice of a china cabinet, as well as a few practical things that you should keep in mind.

  • Budget: Although you may manage to find a good deal on a used china hutch, most of the brand-new vintage china cabinets are relatively expensive. Take an honest look at your budget and decide on the amount that you are willing and able to invest in a new china cabinet.
  • Location: Before you head out to purchase your new china cabinet, you should select a place in your home to display it. Of course, you wouldn’t want to spend money on a large antique china cabinet, only to realize that it won’t fit inside your breakfast nook. Additionally, you will want to choose a china hutch that coincides with the rest of a room’s décor.
  • Style: Choose the ideal style based on your needs. If you plan on using your new china cabinet to display music boxes and porcelain dolls, then you may want to look for a china hutch with glass doors. However, if you are in the market for a basic china hutch to place odds and ends, then you will probably be most interested in china cabinet storage.

Features to Consider

There are a handful of features that you may want to consider when shopping for china cabinets. For instance, a china closet may be preferable if you are just interested in a place to store your holiday dinnerware throughout the rest of the year, without needing to display it.

  • Storage Options: If you want a convenient place to stash your car keys and mail, then you might want to look for china hutches with drawers, since china cabinet storage would be beneficial for you. Small china cabinets are also a good choice if you are buying the cabinet for practical purposes.
  • Display: Now, if you are interested in buying a decorative china cabinet for display, then a china hutch with glass doors or an antique china cabinet would be most appealing to you. Vintage china cabinets also work well for decorative and display purposes.
