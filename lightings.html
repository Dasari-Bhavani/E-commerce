Lighting Fixtures

Providing Many Unique Ways to Illuminate Your Home

Light fixtures are one of the most important items you can buy for your home because lights serve many purposes, including setting the mood, helping read, and security. Selecting the best light for your space requires choosing everything from brightness to the design of the light fixture. You want your room lights to make a statement and show your style off while still serving a purpose. Take time to select your light fixtures, so you can find the best ones for your homeâ€™s design and lighting needs.

Before You Buy

When shopping for modern light fixtures, you want to consider everything possible, including where the light will be installed, what activity happens in the room, and the design of the space. Here are some things to consider before you go out shopping for new lighting fixtures:

  • Where in your room do you need additional lighting?
  • Are there dark spots or shadows in your room?
  • Do you need ambient lighting?
  • Or do you need task lighting for reading or cooking?
  • Do you need accent lighting to create a spotlight on a feature in the room?
  • Will the light be used to decorate the room or act as an accent piece in the space?
  • How will the light be powered?
  • Where will it need to be installed?
  • Do you need to hire an electrician to help with installation?

Features to Consider

Different light fixtures offer various features. Some features are unique to a specific light fixture style, while others are available for most forms of lighting fixtures.

Dimmable

A dimmer switch helps you adjust the brightness of the light. Dimmers work well in living rooms, bedrooms, and kitchens, where you may need bright light to perform some tasks but need dimmer light to create a soft mood in the home.

Easily Adjusted

Lights fixtures, such as track lighting and expandable arms, allow you to move the lighting to focus it where you need light. These types of lights work well in home offices where the lights can be directed to work areas.

Energy-Efficient

Energy-efficient lights are an eco-friendly addition to a home, providing lighting that uses less energy and helps save money.

Motion or Light Sensor

Motion sensor lights are commonly used for outdoor lighting fixtures as a security measure. These lights turn on when motion is detected and turn off after several minutes of no movement in the area. Light sensor lights are those that turn off when another light is turned on. This type of sensor works well in bathrooms and hallways when a small light helps ensure no one trips, but the light is not needed when other lights are turned on.

Spotlight

Some light fixtures allow you to move the light, similar to a spotlight, so that you can focus it on a specific object or area of the room. For example, spotlights work well when highlighting items on a bookshelf, works of art, or point toward a chair commonly used for reading.
