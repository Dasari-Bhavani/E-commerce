Chaise Lounges for Every Home

If you are looking for a relaxing place to sit and enjoy your free time, then there is no better option than a chaise lounge. Sitting in one of these pieces of furniture will give you the feeling that you are floating on air. However, finding the right one can be difficult since there are many different styles available. LuxeDecor has an extensive product line so you can find the perfect lounges for your home.

Before You Buy

Pricing

Buying a chaise lounge is a major purchase and is a serious consideration when shopping for your home. A chaise lounge can cost between $350 to as much as $1000 or more, depending on the design and quality of material used. If you are looking for an affordable option, then consider buying one made from rattan rather than metal which will have better durability but may have lower quality.

For those who want to spend more on a chaise lounge, then you can find ones that are made from higher-quality materials such as leather chaise lounge and stainless steel which will last longer but may have lower durability.

Size

When picking out your chaise lounge couch, you'll need to think about where you're placing it. Will it be in a small guest room? A large master bedroom? You may want to place it in a living room or outdoor space as well. Make sure to take the time to measure the room that you want your couch in so that it fits perfectly and there is enough space to walk around.

You'll also want to consider the size of your lounge. Large chaise lounges work best in big open spaces or outdoors. A lounge with armrests will take up more space than a modern chaise lounge that may have a more simple and sleek design.

Style

Modern chaise lounges are very popular right now as an indoor lounge chair because of their contemporary yet timeless design. Traditional chaise lounges have a more classic look that might be better suited to an office or library. Modern chaise lounges are perfect for any space, especially for a bedroom.

Color is also something that matters when it comes to picking out the perfect chaise lounge for your home. Look for lounges with neutral tones or designs that will match most rooms and styles. You can also go in the other direction and choose a color that will pop and make your chaise lounge a show stopper.

Features to Consider

Material Type

When shopping for your perfect lounge, it is important to consider the material type before making a purchase. Fabric styled chaise lounge and upholstery styled come in linen, velvet, leather, faux leather, and microfiber.

Durability and Weight of the Fabric

You'll also want to consider the weight of fabric and upholstery. The heavier fabrics will last longer but may not be suitable for outdoor chaise lounges in warm weather or other areas where it would get wet.

Enough Space and Seating

It's important that your chaise lounge is big enough for the person sitting on it. If it's for a small child, it will need to be about three feet deep and two feet wide. For an adult, it will need to be at least five feet long and four or more feet deep.

You can even get a chaise lounge for more than one person! There are double wide chaise lounges that are perfect for a couple that would like to rest together.

Additional Features

Do you want a chaise lounge with armrests? Do you plan on using your chaise in the living room where there may not be space for tables? Pay attention to these considerations when shopping for your perfect chaise lounge.
