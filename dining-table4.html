Before You Buy

With so many styles and options available, how do you know which dining room furniture set is right for your home?

  • Budget: You'll want to consider your budget before making a purchase. You may want to buy something simple and affordable for a side room, or you may be looking to buy elegant oak dining room furniture that will last you the rest of your life. We have something for every budget.
  • Styles: What style of dining room furniture do you prefer? Traditional, modern, or transitional styles all work well. You might also want a set that is more rustic and casual if your home has an old-world feel. Contemporary dining room furniture works best for a clean high-end look. Once you narrow down the style of dining room furniture, you can then identify a color palette that is right for your home.
  • Functionality: Here are a few questions to ask yourself before making the purchase: Do I need seating that accommodates large groups of people or just one person at a time? Am I looking for chairs with armrests or without armrests? Do I prefer chairs that swivel or stationary seats? And, finally, do I want a table with leaf extensions to accommodate larger groups of people for holidays and special occasions?

We have a wide variety of styles and colors to choose from, so you're sure to find something that will be perfect for your home!

Features to Consider

Here are a list of features to consider when purchasing a dining room set:

  • Table Shape: Dining room table sets come in a variety of sizes including round, rectangular, and square. The size of your table will depend on the number of people you plan to seat at it for each mealtime.
  • Chair Types: Chairs come with or without armrests; swivel seats or stationary seats; and a variety of colors. They also come in different fabrics, such as leather or linen. You will also want to consider the size of your chairs and make sure that they are not too big or small for the table you are purchasing.
  • Table Extensions: Do you need a dining room set with extensions to accommodate larger groups of people? If so, then it's important that your chairs have arms and can swivel in order to make sure they will fit comfortably around an extended table.
