Wall Mirrors

If you're looking for a serious yet sophisticated piece to add to your home decor, then look no further than wall mirrors. Not only do wall mirrors reflect much-needed light into any room in your home, but they also help to widen your smaller rooms. But first, you should know how to choose the right wall mirror.

Before you can successfully buy suitable wall mirrors for your home, there are a few things that you should consider. This is why the following guide is helpful for you when you start browsing for your wall mirrors.

The addition of a decorative wall mirror to any space is primarily functional, but can also dramatically create the illusion of more space and brightness. Whatever space you're working with, from a hallway, bedroom, or dining room, a well-placed wall mirror can serve to beautify your home with light, polish, and dimension. A well-designed wall mirror, which you can find through mirror wall art, and different shapes like a round wall mirror, or a rectangular full-length wall mirror, goes beyond a simply reflective purpose.

The right wall mirror creates style and enlivens a previously sparse space. Large wall mirrors break space, creating variety on your walls that adds a window of shine and texture to keep your interiors dynamic. In this way, mirror wall décor is more versatile than purely decorative wall art. With LuxeDecor’s diverse collection of wall mirrors, we have decorative wall mirrors to suit any style or décor.

Materials

You'd be surprised to find that before wall mirrors were invented, you had to use pools of water to look at your reflection. Modern wall mirrors are made from glass that has a reflective covering. However, you can find other materials such as metal and plywood used for the frame. Other wall mirrors have a material that forms part of the base. This can be metal.

What about your Style?

If you're planning on buying your wall mirrors, you should consider how they'll match your style. Wall mirrors are an ideal way to showcase your taste. The great news is that there are unique styles that you can choose from to achieve your goals. The thing is, it's always easier to decide on the style that you need if you already have a preference for the types of wall mirrors that you want. However, if you haven't thought about what you'd like, you have a wide selection to choose from. Whether you prefer to keep it casual with standard modern wall mirrors, or you want antique wall mirrors, we have it. If you want something fun, select Hollywood glam-inspired wall mirrors.

Size & Weight

The weight of your wall mirrors matters. You don't want to buy wall mirrors that will be too heavy for your walls. Additionally, you don't want to hang heavy wall mirrors above your delicate and expensive furniture. The general rule of thumb is that you shouldn't choose wall mirrors that are more than two-thirds of the size of your furniture. You should ensure that your walls can safely accommodate their weight.
