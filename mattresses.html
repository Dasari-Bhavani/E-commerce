Introduction to Mattresses

If you have problems with tossing and turning in the middle of the night, you are most likely sleeping on the wrong mattress. The key to a good night’s sleep truly lies in the quality of your mattress. There are a variety of mattresses for sale to choose from at LuxeDecor that can offer you the exact features you are looking for. Depending if you are a side sleeper, back sleeper, or you sleep on your stomach, there’s a wide selection of mattresses for sale at LuxeDecor.

Different Styles and Types of Mattresses

Twin mattresses are perfect for children and teenagers, and in case your children are experiencing a growth spurt, twin XL mattresses are also available that offer extra length. Full size mattresses and queen size mattresses are the most popular sizes and are available in many different materials. From coil to latex to gel and foam mattresses, there are many types to choose from at LuxeDecor. Memory foam mattresses are also available at LuxeDecor. These types of mattress for sale hug and hold your body, and don’t block pressure points. Foam styles from Royal Pedic are perfect for relieving pressure points especially among those who’ve had back surgeries or broken bones. Gel mattresses from Restonic have a cooling effect on the body and help to regulate temperature while you sleep. Gel styles are firm but still help to relieve pressure. Latex mattresses for sale are weight sensitive not heat sensitive, offering quick shape recovery.

The Benefits of Mattresses

There are many benefits that come from investing in quality top rated mattresses. If you sleep on your stomach, you can benefit from purchasing a firmer style to support your body. If you are a side sleeper, you need to choose a style that your curves can fit into so look for average levels of firmness, and consider investing in a plush pillow top. If you are a back sleeper, look for a happy medium of softness. Softer queen styles typically have three inches of latex, medium firmness comes from two inches of latex, and if you are looking for firm king size styles you should select no more than one inch of latex.
