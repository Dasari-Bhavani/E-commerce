Introduction to Bed Sheets

The average person spends about a third of their lifespan in bed, making bedding and sheets a great investment in our day-to-day comfort; you never want to skimp on quality when it comes to sheet sets. Sheets add another layer of style and personality to your bedding. LuxeDecor carries some of the best styles in the industry in terms of quality and comfort provided. From high thread count to premium quality materials, you will be sleeping on only the very best bed covers when you purchase them from LuxeDecor.

Different Styles and Types of Bed Sheets

There are a number of materials that one should consider when buying luxury sheets such as satin, cotton, and silk, and a number of factors including fiber and weave. The weave affects the way that a sheet feels and impacts its longevity. The number of vertical yarns matters as the larger the number, the softer the fabric will be. LuxeDecor is proud to carry high 600 thread count styles in all sizes including flat, fitted, twin, queen, king, and California king. When it comes to material and fiber, cotton is still considered as the go-to fiber. It is comfortable, all year round and easy to care for. LuxeDecor carries premium sheets made from the finest Egyptian cotton woven in Italy, ensuring you are sleeping on only the very best night after night.

Decorating With Bed Sheets In Your Home

There are many ways to decorate with bedding in your home and bedroom. Bed sheets are essential to your bedding for hygienic purposes but are also a great decorative layer. If your duvet cover is patterned, solid styles will be the most stylish complement, but if your duvet or quilt is a neutral or solid shade you can use sheets to add a pop of color, pattern, or print. All sheets at LuxeDecor come in a variety of colors and prints to choose from to give you a chance to showcase your personal style with bedding.
