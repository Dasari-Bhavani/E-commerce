Introduction to Throw Pillows

Throw pillows are a classic addition to your home décor. They are available in many different colors, sizes, shapes, and styles. These accent pillows will give your home a more comfortable appeal. However, you will want to avoid over-doing it. Too many throw pillows can crowd a bed or couch, making them seem uninviting.

So, how should you choose throw pillows for your home? And how do you achieve that perfect balance of comfort without crowding? Well, it’s not too difficult to select the right decorative pillows for your home. You will just need to pay attention to sizes, colors, styles, and designs.

While you can place four or five small throw pillows on your couch without overcrowding it, if you decide to go with large throw pillows, two pillows should be sufficient.

Before You Buy

First, you should consider colors, designs, and styles. Take a good look at the décor and theme of the room where you intend to place the pillows. It may help to snap a few photos to use as a guide when you shop for accent pillows. While these pillows can enhance the hominess of a room, they can appear to be tacky and out of place if they aren’t color coordinated. If your bedroom sheets and curtains are a blue shade, then teal throw pillows or green throw pillows should work fine, but you certainly wouldn’t want to go with orange throw pillows. If your living room décor has been done in a boho style, then you may want to select fluffy pillows or boho pillows to coordinate your décor.

Next, you should consider size and amount. As mentioned before, there won’t be much room left to sit if you have too many throw pillows for couch. However, you may be able to get away with a larger number of throw pillows for bed, even though many of them will likely end up on the floor every night. If you decide on small throw pillows, you may be able to manage several pillows on your couch or loveseat. Just keep in mind that smaller pillows are an excellent choice for decorative pillows, but they aren’t always as comfortable. Large throw pillows and fluffy throw pillows should be used sparingly, but they are more comfortable to lean against.

Finally, you should consider your budget. Since throw pillows are relatively inexpensive items, you don’t need to be concerned about pinching pennies or breaking the bank with this purchase, but once you select the styles and colors that you want, begin browsing to get a feel of the average cost. It is easy to get carried away with decorating your home, but remember that when it comes to throw pillows, less is often more. Look at your budget and decide on the amount to set aside for purchasing accent pillows.

Features to Consider

When selecting throw pillows, there are several features to keep in mind. First of all, the color of the pillows is one of the most important features to pay attention to if you want your home to appear warm and inviting, rather than tacky. Throw pillows are available in a full range of colors. You can choose from pink throw pillows, blue throw pillows, and even white throw pillows, if you want to play it safe.

Texture is another feature of throw pillows. If you are just in the market for decorative throw pillows, then texture may not matter, but if you want pillows that are comfortable to lean against, you may want to go with fluffy pillows or large throw pillows. You have the additional option of choosing pillows that have a velvety texture, floral pattern, or accented frill if style is your main buying point.
